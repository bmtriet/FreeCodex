# DM/Email Outreach Draft

Channel: DM or email
Status: autonomous-use candidate

## Use When

The person has publicly invited feedback or contact, and the message can reference a specific public repo or launch page without scraping private data.

## Message

Hi,

I saw your public project and noticed it may be a fit for a lightweight pre-launch repo safety audit I am testing for AI-built apps.

The checklist covers obvious leaked-secret patterns, public-readiness files, env handling, CI basics, and agent/MCP risks if the repo uses those workflows. I do not need account access or private credentials, only the public repo.

I am offering the first 3 audits free while validating the workflow. The deliverable is a short prioritized markdown report, not a certified pentest.

Would you like me to review your public repo?

No worries if not useful.

## Autonomous Use

- [ ] Recipient publicly invited direct contact.
- [ ] Message is directly relevant to a public project.
- [ ] Sent message logged under `leads/sent/`.
